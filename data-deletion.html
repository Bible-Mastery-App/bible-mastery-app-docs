Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Account and Data

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Bible Mastery App
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Scroll down to Account Management
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion by typing "DELETE" in the confirmation field
  6. Tap Permanently Delete Account

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to: gabrielggibson@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request - Bible Mastery App

Include in your email:
• Your registered email address
• App ID (if known): 717940314290141
• Reason for deletion (optional)

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • ✅ Personal account information (email, profile)
  • ✅ All learning progress and mastery data
  • ✅ Quiz scores and SM-2 algorithm data
  • ✅ Study session history
  • ✅ App preferences and settings
  • ✅ Any stored notes or bookmarks

Data Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Account access is disabled
  • Within 24 hours: Personal data removed from active systems
  • Within 30 days: Complete deletion from all backups and archives
  • Confirmation: You'll receive an email confirming complete deletion

Data That May Be Retained

For legal and technical reasons, we may retain:

  • Anonymized usage statistics (no personal identifiers)
  • Technical logs required for security (IP addresses in logs for 90 days max)
  • Financial records if purchases were made (as required by law)

Important Notes

⚠️ This action is irreversible

Once deleted, your progress cannot be recovered

⚠️ Active subscriptions

Cancel any paid subscriptions before deleting your account

⚠️ Multiple devices

You'll be logged out of all devices immediately

Your Rights

Under privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Receive confirmation of data deletion
  • Export your data before deletion
  • File complaints with privacy authorities if needed

Need Help?

If you encounter issues with account deletion or have questions:

  • Email: gabrielggibson@gmail.com
  • Response time: Within 48 hours
  • Include "Data Deletion Support" in your subject line
